About Government as a Platform

Government as a Platform is a concept that refers to the idea of creating a common infrastructure that can be shared by multiple government agencies and services, making it easier for citizens to access and use government services.

Our Research

Our research focuses on the benefits and challenges of implementing Government as a Platform. We are studying how this concept is being used in various countries and how it can be adapted to different contexts.
